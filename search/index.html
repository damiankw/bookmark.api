Are you looking for quality Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander resources for children and adults?

Our collection is unique! We have over 2,500 thousand resources available for our resource members to borrow.

  • The collection includes picture books, story books, games, activity kits, puzzles, puppets, dolls, artefacts, music kits, non-fiction, autobiographies, CD’s and DVD’s
  • Suitable for early years, middle childhood and adults.

Members can borrow up to 12 items for up to 6 weeks. Resources can be selected online and ordered by email, phone or in person by appointment.

How will it help you?

The collection supports:

  • Growth of cultural awareness and understanding of cultural security
  • Inclusion and well-being of Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander children and families in child care, education and health settings
  • Celebration of Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islanders cultures and peoples
  • Programming and curriculum development
  • Early Years Learning Framework (EYLF)
  • My Time Our Place
  • Australian Professional Standards for Teachers 1.4 and 2.4
  • The development of Reconciliation Action Plans (RAPs)

Our goal is to support Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander organisations, artists and writers and to maintain a collection that is culturally relevant, balanced and reflects the diversity of Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander groups.
